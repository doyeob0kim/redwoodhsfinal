Academic Programs at Redwood High School

Redwood High School offers a wide range of courses to help students excel academically. Our curriculum includes core subjects, electives, and advanced placement opportunities, designed to challenge and engage students at all levels.

Core Subjects

  • Mathematics: Algebra, Geometry, Calculus, Statistics
  • Science: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Environmental Science
  • English: Literature, Composition, AP English
  • Social Studies: World History, U.S. History, Government, Economics
  • Foreign Languages: Spanish, French, Latin

Electives

  • Art: Painting, Sculpture, Photography
  • Music: Choir, Band, Orchestra
  • Technology: Computer Science, Robotics
  • Physical Education: Health, Sports, Fitness

Advanced Placement (AP) Courses

Redwood High School offers Advanced Placement courses for students who wish to challenge themselves with college-level coursework. AP courses include:

  • AP Calculus
  • AP Biology
  • AP Chemistry
  • AP History
  • AP English Literature

For more information on specific courses, please contact the Academic Office or visit the school's course catalog.
