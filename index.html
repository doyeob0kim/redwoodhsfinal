Welcome to Redwood High School!

We are proud to be a vibrant, inclusive community where students are empowered to explore their passions, engage in rigorous academics, and develop as leaders of tomorrow. At Redwood, we foster a supportive environment that encourages growth, creativity, and respect, preparing students for success in their future endeavors. Join us as we continue to celebrate academic excellence, community spirit, and a commitment to making a positive impact on the world around us.

We invite you to explore our website and learn more about the incredible opportunities waiting for you at Redwood High School.

Our Goal

At Redwood High, our goal is to inspire each student to reach their full potential through academic excellence, personal growth, and community involvement. We aim to prepare students for success in college, careers, and beyond, fostering responsible, compassionate individuals who will make a positive impact.

Why Redwood?

  • Strong Academic Programs - Offering a rigorous curriculum, including honors and AP courses.
  • Sports Excellence - Competitive teams in a wide range of sports.
  • Diverse Extracurricular Activities - Opportunities for students to engage in arts, music, clubs, and leadership roles.

Get Involved!

There are many ways to be a part of our school community. Whether it's through our academic programs, athletic teams, or clubs, we encourage all students to find their passions and make a difference.
