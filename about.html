Welcome to Redwood High School

Located in Larkspur, California, Redwood High School is a proud institution committed to providing an excellent education to all its students. We believe in fostering a positive and challenging learning environment where every student has the opportunity to succeed.

Our Mission

At Redwood High School, our mission is to empower students to excel academically, socially, and personally. We offer a wide range of courses, extracurricular activities, and support services to ensure that all students have the tools they need to achieve their goals.

Our Values

  • Excellence: Striving to achieve the highest standards in education.
  • Integrity: Fostering a community built on honesty, respect, and responsibility.
  • Inclusivity: Valuing diversity and ensuring that all students feel supported and welcomed.
  • Community: Building strong connections between students, staff, and families.

Our History

Redwood High School was founded in 1959 with the goal of providing quality education to the residents of Larkspur and surrounding areas. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the leading high schools in Marin County, known for our outstanding academic programs, athletic achievements, and vibrant school culture.
